ASSIGNMENT OF AFTER PAYOUT WORKING INTEREST

This ASSIGNMENT OF AN AFTER PAYOUT WORKING INTEREST is made and
entered into this 3rd day of September, 2013, by and between ZENECO, INC., 6100 S. Yale
Avenue, Suite 1700, Tulsa, OK 74163, hereinafter referred to as Assignor and MESCALERO
MINERALS, LLC, 6100 S. Yale Avenue, Suite 1700, Tulsa, Oklahoma 74136,'hereinafter
referred to as Assignee.

For and in consideration of the sum of Ten Dollars ($10.00) and other good and valuable
consideration, the receipt and sufﬁciency of which is hereby acknowledged, Assignor herein
grants, bargains, sells, transfers, assigns and conveys unto MESCALERO MINERALS, LLC its
successors and assigns, effective upon “Payout” as such term is hereinafter deﬁned, a ﬁve
percent (5%) working interest, in and to any well located on the oil and gas leases covering
lands listed on Exhibit "A" attached to and made a part hereof, on a well by well basis, all
proportionately reduced to Assignor‘s current working interest in the leases. For the purposes
hereof “Payout” shall be deﬁned as the point in time when the revenue from the sale of
production from a well located upon the above described oil and gas leases accruing to the
working interest of Assignor, after deduction of royalty, overriding royalty interests assigned as
of the date hereof, and severance and production taxes equals the cost incurred by Assignor of
drilling, completing, equipping and operating such well to that point in time. Assignor also
assigns all of that certain proportionately reduced ﬁve percent (5%) working interest,
upon payout in and to any well located on the oil and gas leases covering the lands listed
on Exhibit A attached hereto, that was assigned to It by Palace Exploration Company
and/or Roda Drilling, LP.

It is the intent that Mescalero Inc. shall own only a ﬁve percent (5%) working interest in and to
the oil and gas leases described in the attached Exhibit A in and to any well located thereon, on
a well by well basis, upon payout of any well drilled on the oil and gas leases or lands pooled
therewith, proportionately reduced to the interests of Assignor and the previous assignors that
originally assigned the after payout working interest to Zeneco. Such working interest after
payout is to continue to burden the interest of RoDa Drilling, LP and Palace Exploration
Company, their successors and assigns, and is to burden the interest assigned herein by
Zeneco, Inc. Any interest in a well that has paid out prior to the date of this Assignment is
excepted and reserved from this conveyance.

If the above referenced oil and gas leases cover less than the entire undivided mineral
estate in the described lands and/or if Assignor or its previous Assignors owns less than all
interest in and to said oil and gas leases, then the interests herein assigned shall be
proportionately reduced.

The lease acreage hereby is assigned by the Assignor and accepted by MESCALERO
MINERALS, LLC subject to the royalties and/or other burdens out of or with respect to
production with which said lease acreage is encumbered, and subject to the terms of any
agreements under which it may have been acquired by Assignor, and MESCALERO
MINERALS, LLC hereby assumes and agrees to pay its proportionate part of each of said
burdens to the extent that they are a part or remain a burden on the lease acreage herein
