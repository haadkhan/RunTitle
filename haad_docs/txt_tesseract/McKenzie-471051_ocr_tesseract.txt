overriding royalty interest in all oil, gas and other liquid hydrocarbons produced, saved and sold
from the leases described on Exhibit “A”, attached hereto and made a part hereof, LIMITED TO

assigned is not intended to be merged with any leasehold interest owned by Assignor in the

Lease(s)".

and operating expenses, but shall be chargeable with its proportionate part of all gross production
and other taxes properly assessable thereto. The overriding royalty interest assigned herein shall
not be reduced or diminished by the existence of any overriding royalty interest, excess royalty .
interest or other payment out of or with respect to production with which the Subject Lease(s)
is/are burdened or encumbered, and shall not be merged with the leasehold interest owned by
Assignor, either now or in the future. The overriding royalty interest is to be reduced
proportionately to the extent that (I) the Subject Leases covers less than the entire undivided fee
mineral interest, (2) Assignor's interest in the Subject Lease(s) is/are less than the entire
leasehold estate for such leases, and/or (3) the Subject Lease(s) is/are now or hereafter pooled or
unitized resulting in a drillsite spacing unit which includes lands other than the Subject Lease(s).

This assignment is executed and delivered without any representation or warranty of title,
either express or implied, except that Assignor warrants title to be free of all liens and
encumbrances created by, through or under Assignor.

The terms, covenants and conditions hereof shall be binding upon and shall inure to the
beneﬁt of the parties hereto, their respective successors and assigns, and such terms, covenants
and conditions shall be covenants running with the Subject Lease(s) and with each transfer or
assignment thereof.
