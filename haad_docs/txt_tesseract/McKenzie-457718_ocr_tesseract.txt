WELLBORE ASSIGNMENT OF OVERRIDING ROYALTY INTEREST

KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS that the undersigned. RODA DRILLING, LP and
ZENECO, INC., 6100 South Yale Ave., Suite 1700, Tulsa, Oklahoma 74136, hereinafter referred to as
Assignors, for and in consideration of the sum of Ten Dollars ($10.00) and other good and valuable
consideration the receipt of which is hereby acknowledged, do hereby grant, bargain, sell, transfer, assign
and convey unto Richard E. and Deborah J. Sller, Joint Tenants with Right of Survivorship, 4925
East 105th Street. Tulsa, Oklahoma 74137, hereinafter referred to as Assignee, their heirs and assigns, an
overriding royalty interest equal to the undivided net Interest, in and to the oil and gas produced and sold
from the wellbores (“Subject Wells”) as set out on Exhibit "A" attached hereto and made a part hereof
located in McKenzie County, North Dakota.

Said overriding royalty interest herein assigned shall be free and clear of all drilling, developing
and operating costs and expenses excepting taxes that may be applicable thereto. and shall be over.
above and in addition to any previously reserved or conveyed overriding royalty interests. The interests
herein assigned shall be subject to any reversions of Assignors' working interest in and to the Subject
Wells and the overriding royalty interest hereby assigned shall be proportionately reduced upon such
occurrence. ‘ . ’
