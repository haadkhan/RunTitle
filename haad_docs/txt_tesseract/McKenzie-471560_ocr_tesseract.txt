ASSIGNMENT OF OVERRIDING ROYALTY INTERESTS

For consideration paid, the receipt and sufﬁciency of which are hereby acknowledged, MBI
Overrides, LLC, a North Dakota limited liability company, whose address is P.O. Box 7,
Belfield, North Dakota, 58622, (“Assignor”), does hereby grant, bargain, sell, assign and
convey to JRB Investments, LLC, whose address is 1040 12th Street East, Dickinson, North
Dakota 58601 (“Assignee”), Five Percent (5%) of Assignor’s overriding royalty interest
in and to the oil and gas leases covering the lands set forth and described in Exhibit “A”,
attached hereto and made a part hereof.

This Assignment applies to overriding royalty interests owned by MBI Overrides, LLC as of
the effective date and will not extend to any interests acquired by Assignor after the effective
date.

The overriding royalty conveyed herein to Assignee shall be free and clear of any cost and
expense save and except for a proportionate share of taxes and shall extend to any
extensions, renewals, replacements, or new leases taken on lands covered by the oil and gas
leases.

If the subject oil and gas leases cover less than the entire undivided mineral estate in the
described lands and/or if the Assignor owns an overriding royalty interest burdening less
than all interest in the subject oil and gas leases, the interest herein assigned shall be
proportionately reduced. Furthermore, if the leases or any part thereof are pooled into
voluntary units or into units established by any governmental authority having jurisdiction,
then the overriding royalty interest herein shall be reduced in the proportion that the acreage
burdened by said overriding royalty interest bears to all acreage included in any such pooled
unit. The provisions of this instrument shall be binding upon all successors, representatives,
and assigns of the Assignor and Assignee.

In Witness Whereof, this Assignment shall'be effective the lst day of January, 2014.
