ASSIGNMENT OF OIL AND GAS LEASES AND BILL OF SALE

THIS ASSIGNMENT OF OIL AND GAS LEASES AND BILL OF SALE
(“Assignment”) is made and entered into this 30th day of April, 2014, but is
effective for all purposes as of the date set forth on the attached Exhibit “A” (the
“Effective Date”).

WITNESSETH:

THAT HKN BAKKEN, INC., a Texas corporation, and Gerrity Oil, LLC, a
Texas limited liability company, both with an address of 180 State Street, Suite
200, Southlake, TX 76092 (“Assignor”), for and in consideration of TEN
DOLLARS ($10.00) and other good and valuable consideration, receipt and
sufﬁciency of which are hereby acknowledged, does hereby ASSIGN,
TRANSFER, SELL, GRANT and CONVEY unto GERRITY BAKKEN, LLC, a
Delaware limited liability company, with an address of 9137 E. Mineral Circle,
Suite 240, Centennial, CO 80112 (“Gerrity" and “Assignee”), its successors and
assigns, all of Assignor’s right, title and interest, (i) in and to the oil and gas
leases described on Exhibit “A” attached hereto (the “Leases”), INSOFAR AND
ONLY INSOFAR as the Leases cover and apply to those lands speciﬁcally
described in Exhibit “A” (the “Lands”), and (ii) in and to the wells described in
Exhibit “A” attached hereto (the ‘Wells”). This Assignment also covers a like
interest to Assignee in and to the associated personal property, ﬁxtures,
equipment and improvements located on the Lands, or on lands and leases
pooled or unitized therewith, relating to or used in connection with the Lands and
Leases and Wells assigned hereunder. The Leases, Lands, Wells and related
real and personal property and property rights assigned herein, are referred to
collectively as the “Property interests.”

TO HAVE AND TO HOLD, the Property Interests unto Assignee, its
successors and assigns, forever, subject to the following terms and conditions:

1. All interests, rights, duties and obligations previously reserved, either
directly or indirectly, by those from whom Assignor acquired the
Property interests;

2. All terms of operating agreements, letter agreements, farmouts,
farmins, and/or any other agreement relating to the Property interests

and/or the sale of oil and gas that may be produced therefrom, whether ~

executed by Assignor and/or its predecessors in title, and whether
recorded or unrecorded;

3. The undivided interest acquired by Assignee bearing its proportionate
share of all royalties, overriding royalties and other lease burdens
created prior to this Assignment.
